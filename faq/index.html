FAQ

When will you add a specific color or brand?

There will always be a short delay after a brand releases new colors or collections, before we can add them to this web app. Before we can add a new color, we need several things including the product name, purchase link, good quality swatch photos, etc. Once we have what we need, though, it’s usually pretty quick to get them into the Library.

New brands take a lot more work, as there are often hundreds of prior and current color releases. The best way to get your favorite brand added to the Library is to request it here. Once we reach 20 requests for a particular brand, that’s when we begin working on adding it. Here’s what we have so far:

  • Holo Taco - added!
  • Mooncat: 1
  • ILNP: 1
