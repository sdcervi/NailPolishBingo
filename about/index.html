About Nail Polish Bingo

This stash tracker web app is tailored to nail polish enthusiasts, to help you organize and catalog your entire collection.

I originally developed this as a passion project for my own use, to keep track of my growing Holo Taco collection. A few other options exist, like the official Holo Taco Tracker and the All The Combos Spreadsheet, but I wanted one that is more user-friendly, works on my phone, and cloud-syncs across all platforms and devices without any headaches. Google Sheets is notoriously terrible on iOS, and I knew I could build a better user experience.
